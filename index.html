About

Yoda is a fictional character in the Star Wars universe, first appearing in the 1980 film The Empire Strikes Back. He is a small, green humanoid alien who is powerful with the Force and served as Grandmaster of the Jedi Order. With this app you can do tranlation from English to Yoda speak. Yoda often orders sentences as Object-Subject-Verb, switches entire phrases and sometimes uses sentences that are different from his normal syntax!.
